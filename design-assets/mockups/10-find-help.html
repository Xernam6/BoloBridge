A Path for Every Family

Navigating the world of speech therapy can be overwhelming. We've curated these first steps to help you find the right direction.

👶

Under 3

Contact your local early intervention services. They offer free developmental screenings and in-home therapy services for toddlers and infants.

Learn more arrow_forward
🏫

Ages 3-12

Contact your local public school district. They are federally mandated to provide evaluations and support services for school-aged children.

💻

Prefer Online

Telehealth speech therapy options provide flexibility and comfort by allowing sessions to happen in your child's natural home environment.

Explore free and low-cost options through university clinics, non-profits, and state grants specifically designed for speech support.
